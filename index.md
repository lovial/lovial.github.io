# About me

<p align="justify">
Welcome here! My name is Laurent Vial, and I'm a [CNRS](http://www.cnrs.fr/en/institutes/inc-chemistry.html) research scientist at the [Institut de Chimie et de Biochimie Moléculaires et Supramoléculaires](http://www.icbms.fr/csap) ([University of Lyon](http://www.universite-lyon.fr/), France). My research interests focus on artificial receptors for biologically relevant molecules with applications as biosensors or therapeutics.
</p>
